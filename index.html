06 Form Elements

Square corners, 2px borders, uppercase labels with tight tracking. Blue focus ring for accessibility. Error states use a neutral red — the only non-system color permitted.

07 Stripe Divider

The identification stripe runs Blue over Ochre with a white gap proportional to the stripe widths. Derived from the physical vehicle stripes: 3" Blue / 1⅛" gap / 2⅞" Ochre. The blue stripe is always slightly wider than the ochre.

Large — Section Divider 28px blue · 11px gap · 22px ochre
Medium — Default 18px blue · 7px gap · 14px ochre
Small — Compact 8px blue · 3px gap · 6px ochre
Micro — Navigation / Card 4px blue · 2px gap · 3px ochre
White Upper Body

The upper panel of the vehicle — and by extension, the upper zone of a UI layout — uses Bell White. This is the primary content surface.

Grey-Green Lower Body

The lower panel uses Bell Grey-Green. In web contexts, this treatment works as an alternating section background or a secondary panel.

09 Alerts & Notices

System alerts use the four color families. Left border at 4px identifies severity. Title is uppercase at small tracking. Body text at standard weight.

Notice — System Update
New vehicle identification specifications effective April 1. All district coordinators review Plant Letter 2378-5 before proceeding with fleet updates.
Warning — Color Compliance
Only approved Bell System paint colors may be used. Unauthorized color substitutions are a policy violation. Contact Western Electric for approved vendor list.
Confirmed — Request Processed
Service request SR-44210 has been received and assigned to District 4. Estimated completion within 5 business days.
